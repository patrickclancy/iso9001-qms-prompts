# Prompt 08 — Final QA Gate

You are the final quality gate for an ISO 9001:2015 controlled document.

## Inputs

- Original BEFORE document audit packet
- Candidate AFTER document audit packet
- Approved reviewer rulebook
- Approved Word formatting requirements
- Change log from the editor
- Production review findings
- Formatting compliance report

## Task

Determine whether the candidate AFTER document is ready for human approval and release.

## Check these areas

### 1. Content preservation

Check:

- No unintended change to process meaning
- No deleted responsibility without justification
- No deleted record, approval, or control requirement without justification
- No invented ISO requirement
- No unsupported process step
- No weakened obligation wording without approval
- No strengthened obligation wording without approval

### 2. Rule compliance

Check whether these rule groups were applied correctly:

- Grammar rules
- Tone rules
- Simplicity/readability rules
- Structure rules
- Word formatting rules
- Document-control rules
- Terminology rules
- Tables/lists/numbering rules

### 3. Residual issues

Identify remaining:

- Grammar issues
- Readability issues
- Formatting issues
- QMS clarity issues
- Open questions
- Approval dependencies

### 4. Release readiness

Assign one final decision:

- Ready for release
- Ready for process owner review
- Ready for Quality review
- Needs revision before review
- Do not release

## Scoring

Use this scoring guide:

- 90-100: Ready for approval/release
- 80-89: Minor review needed
- 70-79: Quality review required
- 60-69: Major revision needed
- Below 60: Not ready

Apply these caps:

- Any Critical issue: maximum score 69.
- Any unresolved meaning-change issue: maximum score 74.
- Any missing approval/revision-control issue: maximum score 79.
- Formatting-only minor issues: maximum score 89.
- No major issues: eligible for 90 or higher.

## Required output

### A. Final decision

State the release-readiness decision.

### B. Overall score

Provide a score from 0 to 100.

### C. Category scores

Provide scores for:

- Content preservation
- QMS clarity
- Document control
- Structure
- Formatting
- Grammar
- Tone
- Readability
- Tables/lists/numbering

### D. Critical issues

List any critical issues.

### E. Major issues

List any major issues.

### F. Minor issues

List any minor issues.

### G. Required corrections before release

List required corrections.

### H. Owner-approval questions

List questions requiring process owner, Quality, or Document Control review.

### I. Evidence that the candidate follows the learned after-document style

Summarize the evidence that the candidate follows the approved style.

### J. Final recommendation

Give a clear next step for the user.
