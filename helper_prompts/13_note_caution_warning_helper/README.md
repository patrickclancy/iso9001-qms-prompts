# README — Note, Caution, and Warning Helper

## What this helper does

Use this to polish notes, cautions, warnings, and reminders without changing the level of risk or required action.

## Do not use it for

Do not use this to downgrade warnings or turn requirements into optional suggestions.

## What to prepare

Prepare the current note/caution/warning text, the current label, and any risk or control context.

## How to use

1. Open `prompt.md`.
2. Copy the full prompt.
3. Paste it into your AI tool.
4. Fill in the input block at the bottom.
5. Review the proposed wording.
6. Copy only the approved wording into the Word document.

## What to check before accepting the output

Check that risk level and required action were not weakened.

## Recommended next step

Use the meaning guard helper if the proposed wording changes obligations, responsibilities, approvals, records, scope, or process sequence.
