# README — Paragraph-to-Steps Helper

## What this helper does

Use this to turn a long paragraph into numbered procedure steps while preserving the original sequence and controls.

## Do not use it for

Do not use this when the paragraph is a definition, policy statement, legal statement, or non-sequential explanation.

## What to prepare

Prepare the paragraph and any known role names, sequence constraints, records, or approvals.

## How to use

1. Open `prompt.md`.
2. Copy the full prompt.
3. Paste it into your AI tool.
4. Fill in the input block at the bottom.
5. Review the proposed wording.
6. Copy only the approved wording into the Word document.

## What to check before accepting the output

Check that every original action appears in the new steps and that no new step was invented.

## Recommended next step

Use the meaning guard helper if the proposed wording changes obligations, responsibilities, approvals, records, scope, or process sequence.
