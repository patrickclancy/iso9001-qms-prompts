# README — Policy Statement Helper

## What this helper does

Use this to polish a policy statement or short management commitment while keeping the statement clear and controlled.

## Do not use it for

Do not use this to write detailed process steps, work instructions, or new compliance commitments.

## What to prepare

Prepare the current policy statement, document type, any approved policy wording, and terms that must be preserved.

## How to use

1. Open `prompt.md`.
2. Copy the full prompt.
3. Paste it into your AI tool.
4. Fill in the input block at the bottom.
5. Review the proposed wording.
6. Copy only the approved wording into the Word document.

## What to check before accepting the output

Check that the statement did not add unsupported commitments or remove accountability.

## Recommended next step

Use the meaning guard helper if the proposed wording changes obligations, responsibilities, approvals, records, scope, or process sequence.
