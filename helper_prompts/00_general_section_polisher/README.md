# README — General Section Polisher

## What this helper does

Use this when you have a short section or paragraph and are not sure which specialized helper to use.

## Do not use it for

Do not use this for a full document, a full procedure rewrite, or a detailed process map.

## What to prepare

Prepare the paragraph or short section, document type, and any style or terminology rules you want preserved.

## How to use

1. Open `prompt.md`.
2. Copy the full prompt.
3. Paste it into your AI tool.
4. Fill in the input block at the bottom.
5. Review the proposed wording.
6. Copy only the approved wording into the Word document.

## What to check before accepting the output

Check that the text is clearer, that no QMS meaning changed, and that the helper did not add new requirements.

## Recommended next step

Use the meaning guard helper if the proposed wording changes obligations, responsibilities, approvals, records, scope, or process sequence.
